Purpose of ICT and Computers

What is ICT?

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to the use of digital technology, including computers, the internet, and communication networks, to store, process, and share information. ICT plays a vital role in education, business, healthcare, and everyday life.

Purpose of Computers

Computers are essential tools used for various purposes such as data processing, communication, entertainment, research, and automation. They help increase efficiency, enhance productivity, and simplify complex tasks in different industries.

Importance of ICT and Computers

- Improves communication through emails, video calls, and messaging apps.
- Enhances education with e-learning and digital resources.
- Boosts business operations through automation and data management.
- Supports healthcare by storing medical records and facilitating telemedicine.
